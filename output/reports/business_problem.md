# Business Problem: Customer Purchase Behavior & Revenue Analysis

## Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze customer purchase behavior and identify key factors that drive revenue. Businesses often collect large amounts of transactional data but lack clear insights into customer preferences, purchasing patterns, and overall sales performance.

## Problem Statement

The business aims to answer the following key questions:

- Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
- How do customer demographics (such as gender) influence purchasing behavior?
- Which locations contribute the most to overall sales?
- How do seasonal trends impact revenue?
- What patterns exist in customer purchasing behavior?

## Business Goals

- Identify high-performing product categories to optimize inventory
- Understand customer segments to improve targeted marketing strategies
- Analyze regional performance to guide expansion decisions
- Evaluate seasonal trends to improve planning and forecasting
- Support data-driven decision making to increase revenue

## Expected Outcome

By analyzing customer transaction data, this project provides actionable insights that help businesses improve marketing strategies, enhance customer engagement, and optimize sales performance.
