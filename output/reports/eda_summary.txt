# EDA Summary: Customer Purchase Behavior

## Key Insights

- Clothing category is the highest revenue contributor, making it the primary driver of overall sales  
- Certain locations generate significantly higher revenue, indicating regional demand differences  
- Customer purchasing behavior varies across gender, suggesting opportunities for targeted marketing  
- Repeat customers contribute consistently to revenue, highlighting the importance of customer retention strategies  
- Sales show seasonal patterns, helping identify peak demand periods  

## Business Impact

These insights can help improve decision-making in areas such as inventory planning, marketing strategies, and customer engagement to drive revenue growth.
